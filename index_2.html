Who I Am

About Me

Hey! I'm Prakash — a Data Science postgraduate at Brunel University London, originally from India, now building my career in the UK. I started out in Electronics & Communication Engineering but quickly fell in love with the intelligence side of technology — machine learning, deep learning, and making sense of complex data.

What drives me is a simple question: "What story is this data trying to tell?" Whether it's building a full RAG pipeline from scratch, detecting fraud in real-time on Databricks, or predicting crop health from drone imagery — I genuinely enjoy the problem-solving process, not just the end result.

I've had the chance to gain real industry experience — as a Data Scientist at T3 AI Solutions and as an AI Analyst at Ace.Co — where I learned that the best data science is the kind that solves actual business problems, not just academic benchmarks.

"I don't just build models — I try to understand the why behind the numbers. That curiosity is what keeps me learning every single day."

Outside of data, I love exploring how AI is reshaping everyday life — from agriculture and healthcare to intelligent document search. I'm actively open to opportunities across Data Science, ML Engineering, and Analytics in the UK.
